Quick Avacado Toast

Avocado toast is a quick and healthy breakfast or snack option that's as delicious as it is simple. Creamy mashed avocado is generously spread over two slices of golden-brown toasted bread, creating a perfect balance of crunch and creaminess. A sprinkle of salt and pepper adds just the right touch of flavor. This dish is not only visually appealing but also packed with nutrients, making it perfect for any time of the day.

list of ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Directions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl with a fork
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the taosted bread
  4. Sprink sat and pepper on top, then serve immediately
