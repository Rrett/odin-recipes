Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layered pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese, typically mozzarella or Parmesan. Baked until golden and bubbling, it offers a hearty, flavorful meal. Variations include vegetarian, seafood, or gluten-free options.

list of ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • ribbon spaghetti

Cooking Directions:

  1. Layer ribbon spaghetti in pan
  2. mix ground beef and tomato sauce together
  3. sprinkle cheese on the top
  4. cook at 250 degrees for 20 minutes
