Easy Banana Pancakes

Easy banana pancakes are a delightful, homemade treat that combines the natural sweetness of ripe bananas with the fluffiness of pancakes. This stack of golden-brown pancakes is topped with fresh banana slices and a drizzle of syrup, creating a comforting and satisfying dish. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, these pancakes are quick to prepare and a great way to use up overripe bananas.

list of ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of flour

Cooking Directions:

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl until smoooth
  2. Whish in the eggs and mix until combined
  3. Gradually add the folour and stir to form a smooth batter
  4. Cook small portiona of the batter in a pan over medium heat until golden on both sides, then serve.
